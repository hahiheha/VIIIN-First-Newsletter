From Leadership

Message from the Executive Committee

Dear VIIN community,

First of all, thank you for being part of VIIN and for staying with us through both the 'highs' and 'lows' over the past time. Together, we’ve built something meaningful—an ecosystem that embraces complexity and dares to take the less-traveled path.

As we mark four years of VIIN, this month also brings a moment of reflection and renewal. We are undertaking an internal resolution, with a number of changes and new initiatives within the Executive Committee and across the community. One of the things we’re especially excited about is the launch of VIIN’s first Impact Newsletter. Each month, it will curate policy & market updates, new publication, and highlights that we hope will be genuinely useful for you. More than that, it’s a shared platform where we all can contribute content, share events, and funding opportunities.

We’re also happy to welcome a new wave of interns, bringing fresh energy and a strong interest in impact investing. We hope you’ll get to know them at our next VIIN meetup on May 7th.

Looking ahead, we are shaping something bigger than ourselves. VIIN is driving the Ecosystem Building pillar for the Vietnam Impact Alliance, an initiative by GSG Impact. There’s much more to come, and we’re grateful to have you with us on this journey.

Stay tuned!

Vietnam introduces its first policy tying preferential loan rates to ESG criteria, offering 2% interest support to businesses meeting green standards.

Vietnam Secures $72M Climate Finance Payment

Vietnam received nearly $72 million from the Green Climate Fund (GCF) on 28 March 2026 for verified emissions reductions, with the funding set to support forest protection efforts across six provinces.

Banks Expand into Vietnam Financial Hub

Vietnamese banks moved to expand into the Vietnam International Financial Centre (VIFC) in Ho Chi Minh City on 25 March 2026, looking to tap international capital and grow cross-border financial services.

Power Pricing Reform for Energy Investment

A report by GIZ Energy Support Programme Vietnam proposed reforms to power investment pricing, recommending the adoption of a Regulated Asset Base (RAB) model and revised depreciation methods to better align electricity pricing with long-term investment cycles.

Climate Investment Playbook for Emerging Markets

The Climate Investment Playbook, developed by FMO (Dutch entrepreneurial development bank) and British International Investment, provided guidance for VC and PE investors entering climate finance, outlining approaches to align investments with impact frameworks in emerging markets.

Blended Finance Framework for Emerging Asia

A Blended Finance Strategic Framework, developed by UNDP Climate Finance Network and Convergence, introduced six pillars to help governments in South and Southeast Asia scale blended finance markets and improve enabling environments.
